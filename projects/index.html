Walking Wasteland

Walking Wasteland is a Minecraft plugin built on the SpigotMC platform. It grants users a deadly aura that withers plants, kills mobs, and depletes ore near the user, just by being in their presence. Great for griefing, punishing rulebreakers, or just wreaking havoc. Available on SpigotMC and Bukkit.

Also check out the GitHub page for more information or to submit a bug report.
